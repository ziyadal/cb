### Do I pay income tax on property profits?
No. The UAE does not levy income tax on individuals.

Source: https://u.ae/en/information-and-services/finance-and-investment/taxation

### Is there capital gains tax on property?
There is no explicitly defined capital gains tax in official sources.

In practice:
- Property resale profits are not taxed for individuals

### Do I pay VAT when buying off-plan property?
No VAT is charged if:
- It is the first sale
- The property is supplied within 3 years of completion

Source: https://uaelegislation.gov.ae/en/legislations/1227

### Do I pay VAT when selling property?
No. Residential resale is VAT exempt.

### When does corporate tax apply to property investors?
Corporate tax applies if:
- You operate as a business
- You invest through a company

Rates:
- 0% below threshold
- 9% above threshold

### Are individuals taxed on rental or property income?
No, if:
- You are investing personally
- You are not operating under a license

Source: https://tax.gov.ae/

### What property transaction fees should I expect?

Abu Dhabi
- ~4% of property value

Ras Al Khaimah
- ~2% buyer + 2% seller

Dubai
- Transfer fee applies via Dubai Land Department
- Commonly understood as ~4%, but exact rule not explicitly stated in captured official sources
Source: https://dubailand.gov.ae/

Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Fujairah
- Transfer/registration fees exist
- However, a clear consolidated percentage-based rule is not explicitly specified in accessible official government sources

### Are there annual property taxes?
No. There is no annual property tax in the UAE.

### Is there stamp duty?
No traditional stamp duty system exists.

### Key takeaway for investors
UAE offers:
- Zero income tax
- No capital gains tax
- Minimal transaction costs
